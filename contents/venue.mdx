## By Car:
Navigate to the Department of Physics, National Taiwan University: [Google Map](https://maps.app.goo.gl/qRYHGDa2mWmQis7fA)

**Parking Information**

For on-campus parking, you may use the paid parking facilities:
* Sports Center Underground Parking
* Xinnan Underground Parking

## THSR or Train:
Upon arriving at Taipei Main Station, there are two ways to reach the venue:

**By MRT:**

* Take the Tamsui-Xinyi Line (Red Line) toward Xiangshan.
* Transfer at Chiang Kai-Shek Memorial Hall Station to the Songshan-Xindian Line (Green Line) toward Xindian.
* Alight at Gongguan Station and exit via Exit 3.
* Walk for approximately 3 minutes to reach the main entrance of National Taiwan University.

**By Taxi:**

* A taxi ride from Taipei Main Station to the venue costs approximately 210-250 NTD.
